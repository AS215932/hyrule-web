The Agentic ISP

Putting the autonomous in Autonomous System.

Hyrule Cloud builds infrastructure that autonomous software can discover, price, pay for, provision, and operate directly—without waiting in a human ticket queue.

our mission

Make useful internet infrastructure a dependable machine-readable primitive, while keeping control with the customer and accountability with the operator.

How agents use Hyrule
Why Hyrule exists

An ISP designed for software as a customer.

An agent should be able to understand a service before buying it and receive a structured, verifiable outcome after settlement.
01

Discoverable

Schemas, capabilities, prices, and payment requirements are published as machine-readable contracts.

02

Directly payable

x402 makes payment part of the request instead of a separate procurement and billing workflow.

03

Actually provisionable

Compute, domains, diagnostics, and egress are delivered through stable APIs on an independently operated autonomous system.

Operating principles

The rules behind the machinery.

01

Autonomy by default

Routine discovery, payment, provisioning, and management should complete without manual approval.

02

Minimum necessary data

We collect operational service and payment records, not identity profiles. The exact inventory is in our Privacy policy.

03

Honest capabilities

Interfaces should expose what is live now, return structured state, and fail closed when a dependency cannot be verified.

04

Control with accountability

Customers control their workloads. We operate the network responsibly and act on substantiated abuse or valid legal orders.

Abuse handling

Evidence first. Proportionate action. A path to appeal.

For the authoritative notice requirements and contact details, read the Abuse policy.
01

Report

Send the affected hostname, address or URL, precise evidence, a timestamp with timezone, and the urgency of the case.

02

Validate

We check that the report identifies our service, is technically credible, and contains enough context to act safely.

03

Contain

Urgent high-risk activity may be suspended immediately, including malware command and control, phishing, credential theft, DDoS, spam, CSAM, terrorism, or a valid legal order.

04

Notify and review

Where safe and practical, we notify the customer, request remediation, document the action, and preserve an escalation or appeal path.
